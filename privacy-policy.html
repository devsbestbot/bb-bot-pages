🛡️ Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2025

This document explains how our Discord bot (hereafter referred to as the Bot) collects, uses, and stores user data.

📌 Data Collected

The Bot only collects data necessary for its core features:

  • The content of challenge submissions (text, images, or drawings posted by users in the associated threads).
  • Reactions and votes on those submissions.
  • The user’s Discord ID, used to track participation, votes, and calculate scores.
  • The IDs of servers and messages to manage challenge history and prevent duplicates.

The Bot does not collect:

  • Private messages
  • Usernames or avatars
  • Emails, passwords, or any other sensitive data

🎯 Purpose of Collection

Data is used solely to:

  • Generate unique daily challenges per server
  • Display user submissions
  • Enable voting and leaderboards
  • Prevent duplicates and enhance challenge variety

No data is used for advertising or commercial purposes.

🗃️ Data Storage

Data is stored in a secure database accessible only by the Bot’s administrator.
No data is shared with third parties.

⏳ Data Retention

Data is retained as long as the server uses the Bot, in order to maintain challenge history and scores.

Server administrators may request the deletion of all server-related data at any time (see below).

👤 User Rights

In compliance with the GDPR (for users in Europe), you have the right to:

  • Request the deletion of your personal data (e.g., your submissions or scores)
  • Request the deletion of all data related to a server

For any request, contact: devsbestbot@gmail.com, or ask a server admin to reach out on your behalf.

🔄 Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated. In case of significant changes, an announcement will be posted in all servers using the Bot.
